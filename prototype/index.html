Franklin County Family & Community Services are public agencies created by the state of Ohio to provide mental health, developmental disability, and/or substance abuse services to residents of Franklin County and its surrounding areas.

CHILD & ADOLESCENT SERVICES

The Bright Changes program is a comprehensive mental health and substance abuse system of care.

DEVELOPMENTAL DISABILITIES

CHOICES 2 offers a variety of innovative community service programs.

MENTAL HEALTH

These services range from outpatient services, day services, intensive care management, assertive community treatment, crisis stabilization and criminal justice services.

SUBSTANCE ABUSE

A variety of addiction disease services, programs, treatment and counseling is offered at the CRC.

RESIDENTIAL SERVICES

The Residential Department provides housing and support services to adolescents and adults with mental health and/or substance abuse challenges.

Child and Adolescent Services

Bright Changes, is the Franklin County program for children and adolescents. The Bright Changes program is a comprehensive mental health and substance abuse system of care. Bright Changes services will be available to children and adolescents ages 5 years through 21 years. The staff will provide services in both the outpatient clinics and in the community.

We accept Medicaid, PeachCare, or other insurance and can also make other financial arrangements using a self-pay sliding fee scale. For services, call 770-422-0202 and ask for Bright Changes.
